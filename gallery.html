Pinakbet with Bagnet

Pinakbet is a well-known vegetable dish aromatized by bagoong or fish sauce among Ilocanos. With the creativity of the people of La Union, they fused the famous veggie-filled dish with bagnet. The dish becomes meaty, as well! Tourists can enjoy this delectable in every restaurant in La Union.

Basi

The best way to enjoy a beautiful sunset in La Union is to have a sip of Naguilian’s Basi – a wine made from sugar cane. This will surely add to the feels of relaxation after a tiring day of travelling in the province.

Wine of Bauang

Bauang, La Union is celebrated through its vast vineyard where tourist can adore grape-picking activity in the area. The grapes are sweet, yet tourists have to experience wine from these grapes. The grape wines are comparable to famous ones in the country.

Chichacorn of Tubao

A snack that tourist can enjoy whether they are inside of their car traversing La Union or they are at stop experiencing wonderful spots of the province. Tubao’s chichacorn is deep-fried crunchy puffed corn. It also comes in different flavors such as cheese, bbq and caramel.
